How To Play

Welcome to Flesh & Blood!

Click on an area of the card to find out what it means

1.The Big Picture: What's the Goal?

You and your opponent are playing as powerful heroes, battling it out. The goal is simple: Reduce your opponent’s life total to zero before they do it to you.

2.Your Deck, What's in it?

  • 1 Hero Card (your character)
  • Weapons(for attacking)
  • Equipment (gives defense and effects)
  • 60+ cards (attacks, defence abilities, etc)

3.Turn Structure: What Do You Do on Your Turn?

A turn in FAB as a few phases
Start of Turn
Draw back up to your hand size (usually 4 cards unless affected).
Action Phase
This is where the fun Begins. You can:

  • Play an attack
  • Pitch Cards to generate resource
  • Use abilities (some are “once per turn”)
  • Defend against your opponent's attacks (during their turn)

Each card has a pitch value (top left: 1, 2, or 3), which is how many resources it gives when you “pitch” it (discard it to your resource pool). Resources pay for card costs.
End Phase:

  • Arsenal a card (put 1 card face-down in a special slot for future use)
  • Draw back up to hand size
